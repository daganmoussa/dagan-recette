Chocolate truffles

Yields
12 servings 		Prep Time
0 hours 5 minutes 		Total time
1 hour 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 oz.) package Oreos
  • 8 oz. cream cheese
  • 1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 2 cups white chocolate chips, melted
  • 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips, melted

Directions

  1. 1

    Use a food processor to crush cookies into fine crumbs.

  2. 2

    Add all but 2 tablespoons crushed cookies to a medium bowl. Add cream cheese and vanilla and stir until evenly combined.

  3. 3

    Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Using a small cookie scoop, form mixture into small balls. Place on prepared baking sheet and freeze until slightly hardened, about 30 minutes.

  4. 4

    Dip the frozen balls in melted white chocolate until coated and return to baking sheet. Drizzle with semisweet chocolate. Freeze until chocolate hardens in about 15 minutes. Enjoy!

Nutella Pops

Yields
6servings 		Prep Time
0hours 15minutes 		Total time
3hours 15minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 c. Cool Whip
  • 1/2 c. milk
  • 1/4 c. Nutella
  • 6 Dixie cups
  • A pairing knife

Directions

  1. 1

    In a medium bowl, whisk together Cool Whip, milk, and Nutella until combined.

  2. 2

    Pour mixture into six (3-ounce) Dixie Cups.

  3. 3

    Top each with a small square of tin foil to cover cup. Using a paring knife, slice a small slit in the middle of each.

  4. 4

    Place a wooden popsicle stick in each slit and freeze until firm, 3 hours.

  5. 5

    When ready to serve, snip off a bit of the cup and peel off pop.

Swiss roll cake

Yields
10-12servings 		Prep Time
0hours 15minutes 		Total time
1hour 15minutes

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 (15-oz.) box devil's food cake mix
  • 1/2 cups (1 stick) butter, softened
  • 1 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 (7.5-oz.) jar marshmallow creme
  • 1 1/2 c. chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cups heavy cream
  • 24 Swiss Rolls, sliced crosswise

Directions

  1. 1

    In a large bowl using a hand mixer, beat butter, powdered sugar, marshmallow creme, and vanilla until smooth and fluffy.

  2. 2

    Put chocolate chips in a large bowl. In a saucepan heat the cream until steaming and bubbles form around the edge. Pour over chocolate and let sit 5 minutes, then whisk until completely melted and combined.

  3. 3

    Place one cake layer on a serving platter. Spread marshmallow filling over cake and top with second cake layer. Pour ganache over cake and spread it over the cake.

  4. 4

    Apply Swiss Rolls in even rows around cake. Let set 30 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!

Earthquake cake

Yields
10-12 servings 		Prep Time
0hours 15minutes 		Total time
1hour 5minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 c. sweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/2 c. chopped toasted pecans
  • 1 box German chocolate cake mix
  • 8 oz. cream cheese
  • 1/2 c. butter, melted
  • 2 1/2 c. powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • kosher salt
  • 2 c. chocolate chips

Directions

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 350° and grease a 9"-x-13” baking pan with cooking spray. Spread coconut and pecans on the bottom of the pan.

  2. 2

    Prepare cake batter according to package directions, then pour on top of coconut and pecans.

  3. 3

    In a large bowl, combine cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, vanilla and a pinch of salt. Beat with a hand mixer until light and fluffy. Spread the mixture over the chocolate batter then sprinkle chocolate chips on top. Bake until a toothpick inserted into the middle of the cake comes out clean, about 50 minutes.

  4. 4

    Serve warm with ice cream.
